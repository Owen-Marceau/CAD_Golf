Events & Golf Holidays
Pro AMs
I’m excited to offer a range of Pro-Am opportunities throughout the year, with events varying from county-based competitions to regional (South East) tournaments. This gives golfers the chance to play alongside a PGA Professional in a competitive environment on some of the best courses around! You can browse the calendar and sign yourself up directly, with the option to secure your place and pay online for convenience. Each event has details on the format, cost and amount of players required so there is something for everyone. Alongside Pro-Ams, I also run a variety of UK and overseas golf breaks, perfect for combining great golf with quality coaching and unforgettable experiences. The calendar is updated regularly, so be sure to check back often for new dates and destinations!
