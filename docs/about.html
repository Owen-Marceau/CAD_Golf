About me
My Golfing Career
I turned professional in October 2012, finishing off my amateur career at Cooden Beach GC, Sussex. I quickly reached the winners circle, winning my first ever professional event on my first outing, scoring 69 (-3) at The London Club on the Players Tour. Over the years, I have competed on several respected national mini tours, including the EuroPro Tour, Jamega Tour, and TP Tour, and have progressed to the Final Stage of Open Qualifying. My competitive record includes more than 20 career wins at county and regional level in Pro-Ams and Professional events, along with breaking multiple course records across Sussex. I have also enjoyed success overseas, winning both the South Region Overseas Pro-Am and the SPGU Cliff Pluck Invitational in Vilamoura…events I regularly attend on a yearly basis. I’ve been lucky enough throughout my career to be selected to represent the UK in Matchplay events against the Canary Islands, be selected as a Callaway Ambassador for the South Region and represent the PGA South & Sussex in numerous events across the country. For golfers who would like to experience fantastic venues themselves in a competitive environment, I offer the chance to join me in nationwide and overseas Pro-Ams. You can explore upcoming dates and locations by browsing my calendar…I’d love to share the opportunities with you.

Pro AMs
I’m excited to offer a range of Pro-Am opportunities throughout the year, with events varying from county-based competitions to regional (South East) tournaments. This gives golfers the chance to play alongside a PGA Professional in a competitive environment on some of the best courses around! You can browse the calendar and sign yourself up directly, with the option to secure your place and pay online for convenience. Each event has details on the format, cost and amount of players required so there is something for everyone. Alongside Pro-Ams, I also run a variety of UK and overseas golf breaks, perfect for combining great golf with quality coaching and unforgettable experiences. The calendar is updated regularly, so be sure to check back often for new dates and destinations!
